Attaché 0.6.2 reorganizes settings around a clearer mental model and makes updates announce themselves.

## Changed

- **New Agents pane.** The agents Attaché watches now have their own Settings pane. Enabling Claude Code or Codex installs Attaché's status hooks automatically (status updates become immediate) and disabling removes them, restoring exactly what was there before; the old "Precise status" toggles are gone. Resetting your data now also offers to remove the hooks.
- **Integrations means integrations.** The Integrations pane now holds only the external services Attaché connects to with your credentials: xAI, OpenAI, ElevenLabs, Ollama, and custom endpoints.
- **Updates arrive promptly.** Attaché quietly watches its update feed and tells you within minutes of a new version shipping, instead of once a day. You can turn this off in Settings, and the standard hourly check remains either way.

## Install

Download `Attache.dmg` below and drag Attaché to Applications, or:

```
brew install --cask danbryan/tap/attache
```

Requires macOS 13 (Ventura) or later on Apple Silicon. Signed with a Developer ID, notarized, and stapled.
