Attaché 0.6.6 makes watched sessions always manageable.

## Fixed

- **You can always stop watching a session.** A watched session could become impossible to unwatch if it was archived, aged out of the index, or its files were removed: the dock still counted it, but it appeared in no list. Watched sessions now always show at the top of the Command-K picker with a Stop watching option, even when they are archived or no longer indexed.

## New

- **A Watching menu on the dock.** Right-click the link indicator (the "Watching N" chip) to see everything you are watching, stop watching any one, or stop watching all.

## Install

Download `Attache.dmg` below and drag Attaché to Applications, or:

```
brew install --cask danbryan/tap/attache
```

Requires macOS 13 (Ventura) or later on Apple Silicon. Signed with a Developer ID, notarized, and stapled.
