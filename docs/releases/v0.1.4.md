Attaché has a new face and a far steadier voice. This release brings the new Attaché mascot everywhere, a ground-up overhaul of live-call reliability, and a two-way pipeline that proves its deliveries instead of assuming them.

## What's new

- **A new logo.** The Attaché mascot, a hub speaking for its three typing agents, now lives on the app icon, the menu bar, and the idle screen.
- **Live calls tell you what is happening at every step.** Listening, thinking, preparing audio, speaking, sending when the session is quiet, delivering, sent and watching, waiting for the reply with a running counter. No more dead air while you wonder if it is working.
- **Two-way sends prove themselves.** Delivery is parsed from the agent's actual output instead of trusting an exit code, replies correlate positionally instead of by exact text, two-way enablement survives restarts, and delivery fires the moment the session goes quiet instead of on a polling timer.
- **Agent targets are locked.** A send is confirmed and frozen to one session, and mismatches fail closed instead of guessing.
- **Bring your own brain, with a safety net.** Per-task model selection, a live recovery menu when a model fails, and an opt-in fallback chain that steps in mid-conversation when a model runs dry.
- **A Sent tab in the history palette** (⌘Y) shows everything ever sent to an agent, with a jump to each reply.
- **Ambient activity is on by default.** Leave Attaché in the corner and watch what every pinned session is doing at a glance, not just the focused one.
- Fixed a crash when a delivery ran past its timeout, a 400 from providers that reject the "none" reasoning-effort setting, stale call statuses that outlived their moment, and Claude Code deliveries from a different working directory.

## Install

Download `Attache.dmg` below and drag Attaché to Applications, or:

```
brew install --cask danbryan/tap/attache
```

Requires macOS 13 (Ventura) or later on Apple Silicon. Signed with a Developer ID, notarized, and stapled.
