Attaché 0.6.8 restores Echo's real visualizer and fixes captions that got stuck.

## Fixed

- **Echo's full-screen visualizer is the real equalizer again.** The expanded Echo presence had been showing a generic sine-wave bar set that only reacted to loudness. It once again draws the deterministic mirrored spectrum, waveform, and energy pulse from the actual analyzed audio, the way it was designed. Compact Echo, the desktop mini window, and the robot and cowboy presences are unchanged.
- **Captions no longer stick on the first line.** The karaoke caption could freeze on the opening sentence for a whole message while the full transcript tracked correctly, most often after toggling captions or when timing sharpened mid-playback. The caption now follows the spoken word for the entire message.

## Install

Download `Attache.dmg` below and drag Attaché to Applications, or:

```
brew install --cask danbryan/tap/attache
```

Requires macOS 13 (Ventura) or later on Apple Silicon. Signed with a Developer ID, notarized, and stapled.
