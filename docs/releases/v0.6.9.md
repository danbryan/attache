Attaché 0.6.9 makes every presence react to the real audio.

## Changed

- **The real equalizer drives every presence.** The robot and Colt now move their mouths to the actual analyzed spectrum of what is being spoken, not a generic wave, and the Echo presence shows the same deterministic mirrored equalizer at every size, compact or full screen, instead of a simplified stand-in. Nothing is random; the bars follow the real audio. Silent poses are unchanged.

## Install

Download `Attache.dmg` below and drag Attaché to Applications, or:

```
brew install --cask danbryan/tap/attache
```

Requires macOS 13 (Ventura) or later on Apple Silicon. Signed with a Developer ID, notarized, and stapled.
