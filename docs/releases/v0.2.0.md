Attaché gets a soul. This release turns the idle mark into a living companion that shows every agent you're running at a glance, speaks up the moment one needs you, and comes with a new face: Attaché the robot.

## What's new

- **A living companion.** Pin your Claude Code and Codex sessions and they orbit the pet as a ring of motes. Working sessions circle close in; the ones that matter (the session you're focused on, one that needs an answer, one that just finished) move to an outer track where they stand apart. Thirty sessions and one no longer look the same.
- **It speaks up.** A session that needs you turns amber with a question mark and a calm pulse; a finished turn shows a check. Click any mote to focus that session, or hover to see which one it is. Sub-agents ripple, with a live count.
- **A crown that says what's happening.** The space above the head shows the moment: a thinking brain, a turning gear with a timer while a tool runs, a clock counting the wait for a reply, the voice arcs only while actually speaking, and drifting z's when it's resting.
- **A new face: Attaché the robot.** The brand is now the robot, on the app icon, the menu bar, and the idle mark. Prefer something else? Settings lets you pick your companion: Attaché, or Colt the cowboy.
- **A pet on your desktop.** The mini companion floats on your desktop as a calm notification indicator, showing your focused session and any ping, and it resizes from the corner or its Size menu.
- **It talks your way.** Big Picture is the new default personality, and a new Cowboy joins Explainer, Big Picture, and Inquisitive.
- **A softer light theme,** a clearer note when a model has no reasoning levels, and a fix so the "preparing" clock can never get stuck on a busy session.

## Install

Download `Attache.dmg` below and drag Attaché to Applications, or:

```
brew install --cask danbryan/tap/attache
```

Requires macOS 13 (Ventura) or later on Apple Silicon. Signed with a Developer ID, notarized, and stapled.
