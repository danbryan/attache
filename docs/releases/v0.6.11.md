Attaché 0.6.11 keeps the whole conversation in view during a live call.

## New

- **See the whole call, not just the last thing said.** The most recent turn now stays on screen after Attaché finishes instead of fading, and a "Show conversation" chevron opens a side panel with the full running back-and-forth, newest at the bottom and auto-scrolling. Every Attaché turn has a replay button. Open it from the chevron, a right-click menu, or Command-backslash, and pin it to stay open across the call. A private call keeps its transcript in memory only and clears it at hang-up; a normal call keeps its turns in History.

## Fixed

- **The window resizes freely again.** Choosing more caption lines no longer prevents shrinking the window; you can make it small to tuck it out of the way at any line count, and the caption adapts when space is tight.
- **Smoother caption line scrolling.** Adjusting caption lines by scrolling now registers repeated steps from one spot without having to move the mouse between each.

## Install

Download `Attache.dmg` below and drag Attaché to Applications, or:

```
brew install --cask danbryan/tap/attache
```

Requires macOS 13 (Ventura) or later on Apple Silicon. Signed with a Developer ID, notarized, and stapled.
